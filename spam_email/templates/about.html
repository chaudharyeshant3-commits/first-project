About MailGuard

MailGuard was built conceptually to tackle the growing problem of sophisticated email threats. With phishing attacks and malware distribution becoming increasingly complex, standard blacklists are no longer sufficient.

Our platform leverages real-time AI and natural language processing to understand the Context, Intent, and Structure of every email reaching your inbox. This goes far beyond keyword matching.

Our Mission

To provide completely automated, unparalleled inbox safety for regular users and enterprise businesses alike. We believe that you should not have to be a cybersecurity expert to keep your digital life secure.

Technology

Powered by bleeding-edge language models running in the cloud, MailGuard can deeply analyze the sentiment and mechanics of an email to discover hidden credential-harvesting frameworks and scams.
